ToBeBookFair is a little social enterprise aiming to make big changes to children’s reading. We genuinely care about promoting reading for pleasure and the love for books. Think of us as the little independent bookstore on your device. And it isn’t just selling books we care about. We want to put books and opportunities in the hands of as many kids as we can. Our profits will go towards helping schools get book stock. Not just schools though, any organisation that would benefit from a dedicated space and book stock like hospitals, care homes etc. When you shop with us you can choose to use your discount on your purchases or donate the difference to help us help kids.

The National Literacy Trust collected data from 50,376 children between the ages of 8-18 in 2023. The report showed the importance of children owning their own books. Also how most children who own books enjoy reading and read daily.
